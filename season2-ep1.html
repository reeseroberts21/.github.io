S2E1: Hello, Ms. Cobel

Aired: January 17, 2025 | Rambled by Mark Bradley

What Went Down

Season 2 kicks off with a bang—Mark’s innie spills at the gala, freaking out his outie life. Inside, Helly and the team deal with the fallout, while Ms. Cobel, fired but lurking, watches from afar. Reghabi shows up, offering Mark reintegration, and we get a peek at Helena Eagan.

My Rating

10 out of 10—killer start, picks up right where we left off! It’s like Fall Out Boy’s “Immortals”—big, bold, and sets the stage for something epic.

Theories Out the Wazoo

  • Mark’s innie at the gala—dude, that’s insane! What if Gemma’s reveal broke his severance for good? Like, his innie’s out now because seeing her as Ms. Casey flipped something—could be he’s fully reintegrating without even knowing it yet! Maybe that pic wasn’t just a shock—it’s like a key that unlocked his mind, and now he’s stuck with both halves fighting it out—Lumon’s gotta be losing their minds over this glitch!
  • Reghabi’s reintegration offer—okay, she’s sketchy as hell. What if she’s Lumon’s cleanup crew? Like, they sent her to fix Mark—could be a trap to wipe his innie clean or test if reintegration works after Petey’s mess—either way, I don’t trust her one bit! Maybe she’s got some tech up her sleeve, like a reset button, and Mark’s the guinea pig—Petey’s meltdown could be proof it’s a total disaster!
  • Ms. Cobel lurking—she’s not done! What if her firing was a cover? Like, she’s gone rogue, watching Mark to take Lumon down—could be she’s got her own plan now, using his breakdown to get revenge or something! Imagine if she’s been playing them all along, and now she’s free to mess with everything—might even team up with Petey or someone to flip the script!
  • Helena Eagan—finally, the big boss! What if she’s severed too? Like, the Eagans all do it—could be why she’s so calm, her innie’s running the show, and that green MDR glow’s their family secret! Maybe she’s the original, and everyone else is just copying her—severance could be an Eagan thing, and she’s the puppet master pulling all the strings!
  • Helly inside—she’s pissed! What if her speech woke her outie up? Like, her innie’s driving now—could be she’s fully in control, and that’s why Lumon’s freaking out—Helly’s the glitch they can’t fix! Maybe her outie’s been asleep this whole time, and the gala was her innie taking the wheel—Lumon’s not ready for that kind of rebellion!
  • The numbers—man, I’m sure they’re tied to severance breaking now. What if Mark’s gala slip was because MDR stopped refining? Like, they’re the glue, and without it, innies are popping out—could be why Reghabi’s here! That green glow might be fading, and if they don’t get MDR back online, the whole system’s toast—total chaos incoming!
  • O&D’s absence—where’s Burt? What if they’re hiding after Irving saw the weapons? Could be O&D’s gearing up for a fight—maybe they’re the ones who’ll stop Lumon now that severance is cracking! Imagine if Burt’s leading a secret innie uprising—those guns could mean they’re done playing nice!
