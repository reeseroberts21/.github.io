S2E5: Trojan’s Horse

Aired: February 14, 2025 | Rambled by Mark Bradley

What Went Down

Episode 5’s intense—Milchick tightens control, using a “Trojan’s Horse” play to trap MDR. Mark’s outie and innie clash harder, while Helly’s pushing back with a plan. Reghabi’s reintegration offer gets darker, and Helena’s influence grows.

My Rating

10 out of 10—twisty, gripping, pure fire! It’s like Fall Out Boy’s “My Songs Know What You Did in the Dark”—explosive, dark, and hits hard.
