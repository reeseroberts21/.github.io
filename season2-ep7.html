S2E6: Attila

Aired: February 21, 2025 | Rambled by Mark Bradley

What Went Down

Man, sitting here watching Episode 6, “Attila,” I’m totally losing it—Mark Scout’s innie and outie are smashing together at this creepy secret Lumon facility called Attila, and they’re putting him through some brutal reintegration test that’s got me on edge. Helly R.’s leading the MDR crew in this defiant push, not backing down, while Reghabi’s motives are getting darker by the second. Outside, Devon and Ricken are digging into more of Petey’s files, and Helena Eagan’s pulling these chilling reveals that make my skin crawl. It’s intense, and I’m glued to the screen, trying to figure out what’s next!
